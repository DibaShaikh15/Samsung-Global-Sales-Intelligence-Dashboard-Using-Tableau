# Samsung Global Sales Intelligence Dashboard

An advanced Tableau Business Intelligence project that analyzes Samsung's global sales performance, product analytics, customer behavior, and market trends through interactive dashboards and data visualizations.

## Project Overview

This project was developed as part of an Advanced Data Visualization course using Tableau. The dashboard provides actionable insights into sales performance, product revenue contribution, customer satisfaction, regional market trends, and overall business performance.

## Features

### Executive Performance Dashboard

* Total Revenue Analysis
* Total Orders and Units Sold KPIs
* Average Customer Rating
* Monthly Revenue Trend Analysis
* Revenue vs Units Sold Comparison
* Product Category Revenue Share
* Interactive Filters and Drill-Down Analysis

### Product & Market Performance Dashboard

* Global Sales Distribution Map
* Regional Revenue Analysis
* Top 10 Products by Revenue
* Product Portfolio Revenue Contribution (Treemap)
* Customer Satisfaction Distribution
* Region vs Product Performance Heatmap
* Interactive Product and Market Filters

## Key Technologies

To provide decision-makers with a comprehensive view of Samsung's global sales performance and market trends through an interactive and visually appealing business intelligence solution.
